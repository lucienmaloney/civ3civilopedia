#BLDG_Great_Wall
^
^
^Doubles unit combat values versus $LINK<Barbarians=GCON_Barbarians>. 
^
^
^Places a $LINK<Wall=BLDG_Walls> in each city of the continent on which it is built. 
^
^
^This Great Wonder can become a $LINK<tourist attraction=GCON_Tourist_Attraction>.
^
^
^May trigger $LINK<Golden Age=GCON_Golden_Age> for Militaristic or Industrious civilizations.
