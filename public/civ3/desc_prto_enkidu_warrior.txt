#DESC_PRTO_Enkidu_Warrior
^
^
^The Enkidu Warrior is named for Enkidu, a near-savage man created by the Sumerian god Anu to accompany and aid Gilgamesh 
on his quest for immortality.  Enkidu was a fierce warrior and prophet, and the Sumerian armies strove to emulate his 
ferocity in battle and his iron loyalty to the King.
