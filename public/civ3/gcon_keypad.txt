#GCON_Keypad
Number Keypad
^
^
^With your Num Lock "on," you can use the number keypad to move your units. Think of the numbers as the
eight directions on a compass.
^
^1 = move Southwest
^2 = move South
^3 = move Southeast
^4 = move West
^6 = move East
^7 = move Northwest
^8 = move North
^9 = move Northeast
